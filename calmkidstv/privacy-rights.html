管理您的数据并行使您的隐私权

Welcome to the CalmKidsTV Privacy Rights Center. Here, you can submit requests regarding the personal information we collect from you. All requests will be handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

欢迎来到 CalmKidsTV 隐私权利中心。您可以在此提交与我们收集的您的个人信息相关的请求。所有请求都将根据适用的数据保护法进行处理。

How to Submit a Request

如何提交请求

To ensure we can verify and process your request efficiently, please send an email to zengfeiyue2@gmail.com and include the following information:

为确保我们能够高效地验证和处理您的请求，请发送电子邮件至 zengfeiyue2@gmail.com，并包含以下信息：

  1. Subject Line (邮件主题):
    Please use a clear subject line, such as "Data Deletion Request," "Data Access Request," or "Privacy Rights Inquiry."
    请使用清晰的邮件主题，例如“数据删除请求”、“数据访问请求”或“隐私权利咨询”。
  2. Your Account Information (您的账户信息):
    The email address associated with your CalmKidsTV account.
    与您的 CalmKidsTV 账户关联的电子邮件地址。
  3. Details of Your Request (您的请求详情):
    Clearly describe the action you would like us to take (e.g., "I would like to request a copy of my personal data," or "I would like to permanently delete my account and all associated data").
    请清晰地描述您希望我们执行的操作（例如，“我希望请求一份我的个人数据副本”，或“我希望永久删除我的账户及所有相关数据”）。

Types of Requests You Can Make

您可以提出的请求类型

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

    访问您的数据： 请求一份我们持有的关于您的个人信息的副本。

  • Update or Correct Your Data: Request to correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.

    更新或更正您的数据： 请求更正您个人信息中的任何不准确之处。

  • Delete Your Data: Request the deletion of your account and personal information.

    删除您的数据： 请求删除您的账户和个人信息。

  • US Resident Rights: If you are a resident of certain US states (e.g., California, Colorado), you can exercise your specific rights, such as the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your data.

    美国居民权利： 如果您是某些美国州（例如加利福尼亚州、科罗拉多州）的居民，您可以行使您的特定权利，例如选择不出售或共享您的数据。

Verification Process

验证流程

For your protection, we will need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request. We will do this by responding to the email address registered with your account.

为了保护您的信息安全，在处理您的请求之前，我们需要验证您的身份。我们将通过向您账户注册的邮箱发送回复来进行验证。
