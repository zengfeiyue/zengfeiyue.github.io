What Are Cookies and Similar Technologies?

什么是 Cookie 和类似技术？

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device by websites that you visit. Mobile applications, like ours built with Flutter, do not use traditional browser cookies. Instead, they use similar technologies such as secure local storage and on-device identifiers to achieve the same purposes. This policy refers to all of these technologies collectively as "Cookies."

Cookie 是您访问的网站放置在您的计算机或移动设备上的小型文本文件。像我们这样使用 Flutter 构建的移动应用程序不使用传统的浏览器 Cookie。相反，它们使用类似的技术，如安全本地存储和设备上的标识符来实现相同的目的。本政策将所有这些技术统称为“Cookie”。

How We Use Cookies

我们如何使用 Cookie

We use Cookies for the following essential purposes:

我们将 Cookie 用于以下基本目的：

  • To operate our Services: Some Cookies are strictly necessary to allow us to operate our application, primarily for user authentication and security.
    运营我们的服务：某些 Cookie 对于我们运营应用程序是绝对必要的，主要用于用户身份验证和安全。
  • To store your preferences: We use Cookies to remember your settings and preferences on your device, so you don’t have to re-enter them each time you use the app.
    存储您的偏好：我们使用 Cookie 在您的设备上记住您的设置和偏好，这样您就无需在每次使用应用时重新输入它们。

How to Control These Technologies

如何控制这些技术

Because we only use Cookies that are essential for the app's functionality, there is no option to disable them without affecting your ability to log in and use the app. The information is stored locally and can be cleared by logging out or uninstalling the application.

由于我们仅使用对应用功能至关重要的 Cookie，因此无法在不影响您登录和使用应用的情况下禁用它们。这些信息存储在本地，可以通过退出登录或卸载应用程序来清除。
